From student analyst to Senior Advisor in under two years.

The full chronology — every role, promotion, credential, and major engagement milestone. Annotated with what actually happened, not what looked good on paper.

SEP 2020 — MAY 2024
BBA Finance — Misr International University, Cairo
Four years studying finance with a focus on capital markets, valuation theory, and corporate finance. Thesis: "The Effect of Institutional Quality on Innovation" — an analysis of how regulatory and institutional environments shape economic and corporate innovation capacity across countries. Outside the classroom: AIESEC leadership roles, Model United Nations competition and debate, and TEDx event organization — building the ability to operate in rooms and arguments, not just spreadsheets.
Education
SUMMER 2023
Finance internships — two major Egyptian banks, Cairo
Completed back-to-back internships at two of Egypt's largest banks, gaining exposure to retail banking operations, credit underwriting, and corporate banking processes. Also completed the Financial Modeling & Equity Valuation workshop run by Al Ahly Pharos Securities Brokerage (March–May 2023) — the foundational technical training that preceded the CFA Research Challenge work.
Internship
FEB 2024
Finalist — CFA Institute Research Challenge, CFA Society Egypt
Competed as part of the MIU team against teams from the top five universities in Egypt. The competition requires full equity research on a publicly listed company — industry analysis, competitive positioning, financial modeling, valuation, and a final investment recommendation. Recognized with the Outstanding Investment Research certificate from CFA Society Egypt, and a Certificate of Recognition from MIU for outstanding performance. The first real public test of whether the analytical framework built in theory actually holds under competitive, professional scrutiny.
Award
AUG — NOV 2024
Financial Analyst — Fintech company, Cairo
First full professional engagement post-graduation. Developed multi-geography revenue, CapEx, and OpEx forecasts across Egypt, UAE, and US operations for a fintech payments company. Stress-tested unit economics through competitive benchmarking — a useful discipline in how assumptions about user acquisition and churn compound over a five-year model horizon.
Role
SEP 2024 — PRESENT
Independent Financial Advisor — personal practice, Egypt & Gulf
Running a concurrent independent advisory practice since late 2024, delivering financial models, valuations, pitch decks, and financing proposals to startups and founder-led businesses across fintech, healthcare, FMCG, and technology. Work spans Egypt and the Gulf — EGP and SAR denominated. Clients include early-stage companies preparing for institutional raises and established businesses seeking independent valuation opinions.
Entrepreneurship
DEC 2024 — JUL 2025
Financial Analyst → Investment Analyst [Promotion #1]
Joined a Cairo-based investment advisory firm as a Financial Analyst and was promoted to Investment Analyst within the same engagement — the first of two promotions in twelve months. Built dynamic DCF and project-finance models across multiple sectors; prepared investment committee memos and multi-scenario analyses. Led valuation and feasibility work on a major Egyptian financing mandate of EGP 500M — engaging directly with bank credit teams to stress-test the proposed capital structure.
Promotion 1 of 2
JUN — SEP 2025
Financial Analyst — Real estate developer, Cairo
Led financial analysis for a real estate developer's project repricing and payment structure redesign, supporting a sales pipeline of approximately EGP 1.5B. Built sales-enablement financial tools and ran training sessions for the sales team — a useful test of how financial models translate into commercial arguments when the audience is sales, not finance.
Role
OCT 2025 — PRESENT
Senior Investment Advisor — Gulf market advisory (remote, concurrent)
Concurrent remote engagement covering Saudi Arabia and the broader Gulf. Built a 28-slide institutional investor deck for a healthcare-sector client (SAR 826.9M enterprise value). Prepared Arabic-language financing proposals, three-statement financial models, and investment offers for clients in education, security services, catering, logistics, IT, and industrial manufacturing. All deliverables bilingual — Arabic primary, English parallel.
Role
DEC 2024 — PRESENT
Senior Investment Advisor [Promotion #2] — primary advisory role, Cairo
Second promotion within twelve months — from Investment Analyst to Senior Investment Advisor. Current principal-facing role covering deal origination, structuring, valuation, and financial due diligence for large-scale Egyptian and cross-border transactions. Led financial due diligence on an active sell-side mandate: restated balance sheet, rebuilt equity and fixed-asset schedules, and incorporated a property revaluation to support a EGP 296M positioning. Directed financial feasibility for real estate and renewable energy projects including 25-year DCF models for 3MW and 100MW utility solar.
Promotion 2 of 2
2024 — PRESENT
AI Model Evaluator — Outlier (GPT & Gemini)
Concurrent engagement training and evaluating frontier AI reasoning models — GPT and Gemini — across many projects through Outlier. The work requires structured judgment about how language models handle complex reasoning chains, ambiguous evidence, and contested factual claims. The discipline is directly parallel to financial modeling: find the assumption, test whether the conclusion follows from it, identify where the chain of reasoning breaks down.
Ongoing
2025
Judge & Mentor — CFA Institute Research Challenge, Egypt
Returned to the competition I competed in the previous year — now as a judge evaluating investment research submissions from university teams, and as a mentor to the MIU team preparing their entry. The shift in perspective is useful: judging teaches you what makes analytical work actually land in a room of experienced practitioners, which is different from what makes it technically correct.
Leadership
