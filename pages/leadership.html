Competitor in 2024. Judge in 2025.

How the trajectory of the CFA Research Challenge maps the arc of the career — and what happens when you end up evaluating the standard you were evaluated against the year before.

CFA Research Challenge

The same competition. Different side of the table.

Competed in the CFA Institute Research Challenge in 2024 as part of the Misr International University team — finishing in the top five universities nationally, with the Outstanding Investment Research award from CFA Society Egypt. In 2025, returned as both a judge evaluating submissions and a mentor to the MIU team preparing their entry. The shift in perspective is significant: judging teaches you what makes analytical work land in a room of experienced practitioners, not just what makes it technically correct.
