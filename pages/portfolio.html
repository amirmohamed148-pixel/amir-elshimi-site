Sector coverage

The breadth of the practice.

Real Estate
Land development, residential & hotel projects
New Heliopolis land-share joint ventures with total revenue projections of EGP 5.69B and EGP 11.52B. Hotel investor decks near the Grand Egyptian Museum (26% IRR, EGP 1.9B NPV; $58.7M investment, 20% IRR). Payment-structure redesigns supporting a ~EGP 1.5B sales pipeline for a Cairo developer. Multiple feasibility studies for greenfield and expansion projects.
Renewable Energy
Utility solar and green ammonia
Full 25-year DCF models for utility-scale solar at both 3MW and 100MW capacity — project-finance structure with debt service coverage ratios and levelized cost of energy sensitivity. Parallel work on the financial feasibility of a green ammonia production facility, a cross-border industrial project with international project partners.
Healthcare
Hospitals, medical equipment, digital health
28-slide institutional investor deck for a 100-bed Gulf hospital project at SAR 826.9M enterprise value — three-statement model, sensitivity analysis, QC review. Investment committee memo and valuation opinion for a separate hospital project. Financial model, market research, and business plan for a digital health/telemedicine startup. Medical equipment cost schedule for a cardiac and lung hospital.
Hospitality
Hotels and tourism infrastructure
Multiple hotel investment mandates: a 260-room hotel and furnished apartments near the Grand Egyptian Museum ($58.7M, 20% IRR); a separate pre-concept hotel deck (NU Khan) near GEM; and a US-market LBO assessment of a 56-key boutique hotel in Idaho with GP/LP waterfall distribution and IRR hurdle analysis — demonstrating cross-border underwriting discipline.
Technology & IT
Digital transformation, cybersecurity, managed IT
27-slide investment offer deck and full contracts-backlog model (SAR 262M+) for a Saudi IT and digital transformation company with Apple Premium Education Partner status and an MVNO license — projecting revenue from SAR 380M (2026) to SAR 774M (2030). Separate 60-month financial model for a Saudi IT company covering government, large enterprise, and SME customer segments across custom software, AI, cybersecurity, cloud, and managed services.
Industrials & Manufacturing
PPR/PVC pipe manufacturing, due diligence
Full financing study and comprehensive valuation for a Saudi PPR/PVC/HDPE pipe manufacturer: IFRS SME-audited financials reviewed, 3-scenario financial model (base/upside/capacity-ramp), revenue growth from SAR 0.39M to SAR 17.89M (279% growth 2024–2025). Target equity SAR 65–85M, IRR 32.63%, payback 2 years. "Strong Buy" recommendation. Sell-side due diligence on a separate industrial mandate surfacing two irreconcilable valuation figures.
Logistics
3PL, last-mile delivery, B2B distribution
Comparable-company valuation (EV/EBITDA 6.5x–9.0x corridor) for a Saudi last-mile logistics startup — equity value SAR 106–146M (2025E), SAR 182–252M (2026E). Shawarma market analysis covering 169 chain branches in Riyadh as sector context. Separate 3PL financial model and interactive dashboard for a global 3PL client.
Fintech & Payments
Payments, recycling fintech, revenue modeling
Multi-geography revenue, CapEx, and OpEx model for a fintech payments company spanning Egypt, UAE, and US operations. Full 5-year three-statement financial model for a recycling fintech platform: revenue trajectory from EGP 1.5M to EGP 909M across 5 years. Unit economics stress-testing via competitive benchmarking across both engagements.
Consumer / FMCG
Automotive, restaurants, catering, fresh produce
Egyptian family automotive conglomerate: full financial analysis across six entities (Motors, Trade, International Automotive, Telecom, travel), with 4-year ratio analysis, ROA 43%, ROE 45.7%, CAGR 64.9% revenue growth 2020–2023. Izakaya restaurant (Egypt): revenue EGP 27.8M–91.6M, net margin 14–21%. Hatmya Restaurants (Saudi, 10 branches): replacement-cost valuation. Emdad Catering greenfield: NPV SAR 97.7M, IRR 67.9%.
Education & Services
Schools, security services, BPO
Financial modeling and investor offer documents for a Saudi schools group (Fajr Al Shamal) and a Saudi private security services company (Saher Al Shamal). Sell-side financial due diligence for an Egyptian BPO and call-center operator: audited financial review, tax documentation, and a restated EGP 296M valuation.
